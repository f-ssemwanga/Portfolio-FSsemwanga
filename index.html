About Me

Welcome to my portfolio page! My name is Francis Ssemwanga, and I am a Computer Science enthusiast currently pursuing a Master's degree in Computer Science with Artificial Intelligence from the prestigious University of York, England.

As an ambitious and dedicated individual, I am also completing a 16-week boot camp with Purple Beard LTD, a leading technology training company. In addition, I am currently working as a Lead Teacher of Computer Science and Director of IT at SMCS School, where I have developed a passion for inspiring and guiding the next generation of technologists.

My expertise lies in software design and development, artificial intelligence, and machine learning. I am always eager to explore and enhance my knowledge of cutting-edge technologies such as computer vision and language processing, which I believe hold the key to unlocking endless possibilities in the tech industry.

Aside from my academic pursuits, I hold a Bachelor of Science in Education with Maths and a Diploma in Electrical and Electronic Engineering. Furthermore, I have been awarded the GTC qualified Teaching Status, which showcases my commitment to delivering the highest quality education to my students.

I am confident that my diverse skill set, passion for technology, and drive to succeed make me an excellent candidate for any organisation seeking a talented and dedicated Computer Science professional. Thank you for taking the time to view my portfolio, and I look forward to connecting with you soon!
